words to pierce the beyond. words to which I will survive

I'm dying

"Is it blissful?"

It's like a dream

"I want to dream"

""What are you waiting for, my brother, flowering with old age,

All burdened with the chimeras of the world?

(...)

You, who watch your soul ignite,

From the spark of an infinite tenderness

That connects each heartbeat

Like arcs of light,

That makes, in words scattered with abandonment,

Immense universes arise...

So fall, what are you waiting for?""

""But look where you've taken me, sinister chimera

In my hands I hold the treasures of skies and seas

There I see my boat drifting far from shores""

""Sometimes, the trees sketch a curious ballet,

Mimicking my small body rocked by the wind,

And the ivy whispers familiar words,

Casting upon life a child's gaze

To inhale the sweet perfumes exhaled

From a garden whose secret you once took,

Then sang the laughing diaphanous daughters

Of the shimmering, pearly river""

""Those distant lands,

Where tall grasses in vermilion fields

Forever waltz with the light;

Where freely wander

All memories of our lives on Earth

That vengeful time would have wished to erase.""

""It's a vicious orchestra playing tonight, its popular symphony

It knows how to scratch the sensitive strings of sleeping slugs

These hideous oozing wounds that drive me tonight

To join, despite myself, these musicians of the dump""

""This Night is ugly, empty and dirty

Of dead Flames and vomited Sorrows.

So, may the broken Song of our Cries,

Awaken the Stars, with consumed Hearts.

At the Twilight of the World,

Tear away the sand of Tears.""

""Dans un vieux puits hanté, creux et sombre

Résonnaient d'immenses choeurs éthérés

Des échos lointains de mourants archanges

Brisant leurs pures ailes aux récifs

Tombant dans l'éternité comme en décembre

Lorsque la neige s'effondre des cieux tristes""

""En toi se bousculent et se renversent

La poésie des catastrophes

Et le venin de l’urgence

De reprendre le ciel,

De répondre à la morsure par les fleurs du sang,

Et les couleurs de la perte.

Par les vers d’un amour déchaîné.

Et de crier à la mort

Des phrases percées de lumières.

Vomir et recouvrir la nuit.

De mots à pourfendre l’au-delà.

Des mots, auxquels je survivrai.""
